01 · Who we are
We are a group of people with the audacity to build and the courage to stand against the world and say — I'd rather work for myself.

EDC is a startup incubator at Ramaiah Institute of Technology. But that's only half the truth. What we really are is a small, determined group of builders — people who refuse to be normal, refuse to be quiet, refuse to spend their twenties waiting.

We're dreamers. We're operators. We're the ones asking uncomfortable questions in lectures and sketching pitch decks between them. If you've ever felt out-of-place doing the ordinary thing, this is where you belong.
